The Suds

Unite, Defend, Forgive


Step into the vibrant world of Suds - a place where strategy, camaraderie, and cuteness converge. You are a Sud - a charming, cute, and courageous being tasked with the defence of your home. Your village, a lively settlement nestled in a lush green valley, brimming with oversized mushrooms, tall flowers, and hollow tree trunks, is under threat from envious enemy slimes. Only you and your allies have the power to thwart these nefarious invaders. But beware, not all is as it seems...
